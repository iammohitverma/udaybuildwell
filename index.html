PUBLIC NOTICE

Whereas, we Uday Buildwell Private Limited are in the process of undertaking of development of Commercial Colony (under left over land pocket policy) in village Kanhai, Sector-45, District Gurugram, Haryana, over an area measuring 0.375 acres (in addition to 0.65 acres, licence no. 204 of 2022 Dt. 13.12.2022) in accordance with the license granted for an Commercial Colony, licence no. 189 of 2023 Dt. 15.09.2023 by Director General, Town & Country Planning, Haryana, Chandigarh. And whereas, the Building Plan earlier approved at the time of grant of license for the said colony is now proposed to be revised on account of request made by Uday Buildwell Private Limited for revision of Building Plan of the said colony. And whereas, the office of Director, Town & Country Planning, Haryana, Chandigarh has required seeking of objections against the revision of Building Plan, as a pre-condition for allowing such revision in the Building Plan. Accordingly vide this public notice objections are hereby invited from any of the public in the said colony on the proposed revision of the Building Plan of the said colony. A copy of the earlier approved Building Plan bearing memo no. ZP-1712/PA(DK)/2023/9627, dated 05.04.2023 & Zoning Plan bearing drawing no. 8845, dated 14.12.2022 and the Building Plan now proposed to be revised (in principally approved) bearing memo no. ZP-1712/PA(DK)/2023/38531, dated 10.11.2023 & Approved Demarcation cum Zoning Plan bearing drawing no. 9607, dated 15.09.2023 is available for perusal at our office and also on website i.e. https://www.udaybuildwell.com and also displayed at our site office for information. The said Revised Building Plan can also be perused at the office of undersigned as well as in the office of Senior Town Planner, Gurugram. Any public having any objection on the proposed revision in the approved Building Plan, may file his objection in the office of Senior Town Planner, Gurugram, within 30 days of the publication of this notice, failing which it shall be assumed that there are no objections to the proposed revision of the Building Plan. Place: Gurugram Date: November 15, 2023 Subhash Rustogi (Auth. Representative)
